Buffalo Chicken Poutine

An easy mix of carbs and protien to make quickly in an air fryer!

Ingredients:

  • Frozen fries
  • Froxen chicken of your choice
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Ranch dressing

Steps:

  1. Put your liking of fries and chicken into the air fryer
  2. Mix them around
  3. Put airfryer to 200 degrees F for 7 minutes
  4. Shake the airfryer after 7 minutes, and put in for 7 more minutes
  5. When finished, put on a plate with cheese on top, drizzle buffalo sauce and ranch
  6. Enjoy!
