Blueberry Protein Shake

A yummy blueberry shake perfect for after a workout!

Ingredients:

  • A scoop of frozen blueberries
  • One banana
  • A scoop of peanut butter
  • A scoop of oats
  • A scoop of chia seeds
  • Two scoops of chocolate protein powder
  • A cup of milk
  • A handful of fresh blueberries

Steps:

  1. Get any blender
  2. Put all ingredients into the blender
  3. Blend for 20-30 seconds
  4. Pour into a large glass
  5. Top with fresh blueberries
  6. Enjoy!
