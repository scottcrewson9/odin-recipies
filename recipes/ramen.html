Easy Instant Ramen

A twist on the classic instant ramen!

Ingredients:

  • A packet of any instant ramen
  • Two eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • Soy sauce
  • Green Onions

Steps:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Put the ramen in the boiling water
  3. In a bowl add an egg yolk, mayonaise and the ramen seasoning and mix
  4. Once the ramen is ready, take half a cup of the water and add to the bowl
  5. Strain the rest of the noodles and put in the broth, mix
  6. Boil an egg and cut in half to put on top, as well as chopped green onions
  7. Enjoy!
